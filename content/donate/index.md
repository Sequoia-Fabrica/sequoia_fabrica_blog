## Support Sequoia Fabrica

As a volunteer-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit, we rely on the generosity of our community to keep our textile workshop, 3D printers, laser cutter, woodworking equipment, electronics lab, and arts supplies accessible.

## Make a One-Time Donation

Every contribution, big or small, makes a meaningful impact on our maker community. Your one-time donation helps us keep our doors open.

[Donate Now](http://donate.sequoiafabrica.org/)

## Pay Your Member Dues

Are you a Sequoia Fabrica member? Keep your membership active and continue enjoying 24/7 access to our workshop, tools, and creative community.

[Pay Member Dues](https://opencollective.com/sequoia-fabrica/contribute/member-dues-68571)

## Employer Matching Donations

Corporate matching programs are a powerful way to maximize support and double your impact! Check if your employer participates through platforms like:

- Benevity
- PayPal Giving Fund
- YourCause/Blackbaud
- Direct employer programs - contact your HR department about matching gifts

Search for "Sequoia Fabrica" in your company's giving portal or find us by our EIN, 93-4496044.

If you need assistance with the matching process or have questions about our organization, please [contact us](/contact).

## Give Through a Donor Advised Fund

Donor Advised Funds (DAFs) offer a tax-efficient way to support Sequoia Fabrica while maximizing your philanthropic impact.

How to donate from your DAF:

- Log into your donor advised fund provider (Fidelity Charitable, Schwab Charitable, Daffy, etc.)
- Recommend a grant to Sequoia Fabrica, or use our EIN, 93-4496044

DAF donations help us plan long-term improvements to our workshop and expand our educational programming. Thank you for considering this meaningful way to invest in San Francisco's maker community.

Sequoia Fabrica is a California Public Benefit Corporation and tax-exempt public charity under Section 501(c)(3). EIN: 93-4496044

Your donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. We provide receipts for contributions. See a summary of our impact on the [wiki](https://wiki.sequoiafabrica.org/wiki/Impact).

Questions about donating? We're here to help! Please [contact us](/contact) if you need assistance with any donation method or want to discuss other ways to support our mission.
